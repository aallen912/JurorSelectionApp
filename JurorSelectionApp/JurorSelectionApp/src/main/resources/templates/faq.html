FAQ's

Does this application work with private accounts?

JSA does not work with private accounts. The user needs to have a public account in order to view the tweets.

Who created JSA?

JSA is an application created by Georgia Southern computer science students as their capstone project.

How do you verify your status?

JSA has a verification tab where you can verify that you are a member of the Georgia BAR.

How do you make an account for JSA?

To make an account with JSA, you have to go to the 'Verification and Registration' tab and follow the instructions there.

How do I see a specific tweet from a person of interest?

In the Search Person of Interest tab, you can pull up the url of the tweet and look at it via twitter.

How do I add multiple words to the search by keyword?

In the Search Person of Interest tab, you can search by multiple words by putting a "," between each word.
